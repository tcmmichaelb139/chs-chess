About Us

The CCS Chess Club is a student led chess club which was made possible thanks to a grant from the Carmel Education Foundation. The goals of the CCS Chess Club are as follows:

  • Spread awareness of the various benefits of chess to CCS students
  • Allow students to have earlier exposure to chess practice and lessons
  • Help younger talent grow with challenging and experienced opponents from the CHS Chess Club
  • Improve CCS representation at state-level chess tournaments

We are very thankful to the Carmel Education Foundation for helping to make such a program possible, and look forward to growing the love of chess in the CCS community!
