Welcome

Carmel High School Chess Club is a place where people play chess. We meet every Tuesday from 8-9am in room B205. We allow people of all skill levels to join, play, and compete.

About Us

Carmel High School Chess Club, founded in 2021 under the leadership of Yash Bhargava, has experienced remarkable growth and development. Starting from its humble beginnings, the club has steadily expanded its reach and influence to include a diverse and enthusiastic community of chess enthusiasts. Today, we are proud to announce that the club has found a home at the esteemed Carmel Clay Public Library.

At CHS Chess Club, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant chess culture. We organize and host multiple engaging meetings each week, providing a welcoming environment for chess enthusiasts of all skill levels to come together, learn, and compete. As a testament to our commitment, we have also had the privilege of organizing Gambit 425, a highly anticipated chess tournament that embraces participants of all ages. This prestigious event takes place right within the walls of the library, further strengthening our ties with the community.

With a passionate and dedicated team, CHS Chess Club continues to make strides in promoting the game of chess and creating a vibrant chess community in Carmel.
